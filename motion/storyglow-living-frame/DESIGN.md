# StoryGlow Living Frame Design

## Style Prompt
Warm, whimsical, premium custom children's-book motion. The frame should feel like a printed watercolor illustration gently waking up at bedtime: paper texture, soft glow, quiet parallax, tiny star-dust specks, and no morphing faces or body shapes.

## Colors
- Parchment: `#fffaf0`
- Deep navy: `#17344a`
- Sky wash: `#79b7d1`
- Candle gold: `#f1b942`
- Raspberry coral: `#db6071`

## Typography
- Display: Georgia, serif
- UI: Arial, sans-serif

## Motion Rules
- 2 second seamless loop.
- Characters stay visually stable.
- Move only layers, light, paper grain, and decorative particles.
- No AI-video morphing, no face deformation, no fast camera moves.

## What Not To Do
- Do not use photorealistic effects.
- Do not animate character anatomy.
- Do not add jumpy transitions.
- Do not introduce harsh neon colors.
